Game description

Welcome to the exciting world of fantasy novels, where you can immerse yourself in a unique interactive experience created especially for you. With the help of the OpenAI gpt models, you will be sent on a journey full of dangers and mysteries. You have to choose your path and make decisions that will affect the course of the story. Feel like the hero of your own story and plunge into an exciting adventure world created especially for you.

Features of the game

  • Endless plot options for unique adventures.
  • Worlds, locations and surroundings change dynamically.
  • New dialogs are created in real time.
